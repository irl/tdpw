tdpw: FDL Examples

These are the example scripts I submitted along with tdpw when I wrote tdpw for CS3008 Operating Systems. They show the range of functionality that has been implemented.

  • Single square - In this animation, a single square moves around diagnally bouncing off the walls.
  • Michotte 1 - In this animation, a square travels towards a stationary square, bangs into it, and the second square then moves off.
  • Michotte 2 - In this animation, a square travels towards a stationary square, bangs into it, and the two squares move off slowly.
  • Gas - In this animation, a number of circles bounce off each other and the walls.
  • Picture reveal - In this animation, an obfuscated shape stay still for 50 frames, then moves for 50 frames, while the obfuscating pattern stays still. The shape becomes visible.
